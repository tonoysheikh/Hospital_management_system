ABOUT OF THE Psychologist

"Quality is never an accident. It is always the result of intelligent effort."

  1. Home
  2. Psychologist

The Department of Psychology at LANS Unit Exchange Hospital offers a comprehensive range of psychological services, supporting mental health and well-being for patients across all age groups. The department encompasses several specialized centers, including the Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) Centre and the Child and Adolescent Psychology Centre.

CLINICAL PSYCHOLOGY:

  • Diagnostic assessments and psychological evaluations for a wide range of mental health conditions, including anxiety, depression, and personality disorders.
  • Individual, group, and family therapy sessions tailored to the specific needs of each patient.
  • Psychological support in the Specialty Centers across the Outram Campus, including the National Cancer Centre, providing mental health care to patients with chronic illnesses.

NEUROPSYCHOLOGY UNIT:

  • Comprehensive neuropsychological assessments for patients with neurological conditions, including traumatic brain injury, stroke, and dementia.
  • Cognitive rehabilitation and therapy aimed at improving cognitive functions impacted by neurological disorders.

CHILD AND ADOLESCENT PSYCHOLOGY CENTRE:

  • Specialized services for children and adolescents, including therapy for developmental disorders, behavioral issues, and emotional regulation.
  • Parental counseling and support services aimed at promoting healthy family dynamics and child development.

COGNITIVE BEHAVIORAL THERAPY (CBT) CENTRE:

  • Evidence-based CBT programs for treating anxiety, depression, OCD, and other mental health conditions.
  • Offering mindfulness-based cognitive therapy and other integrative therapeutic approaches.

FACILITIES AVAILABLE:

  • Private consultation rooms designed to provide a safe and supportive environment.
  • Advanced psychological assessment tools and software for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.
  • Comfortable therapy spaces for individual and group sessions.
