ABOUT OF THE OPHTHALMOLOGY

"Quality is never an accident. It is always the result of intelligent effort."

  1. Home
  2. Ophthalmology

The Department of Ophthalmology at LANS Unit Exchange Hospital offers comprehensive eye care services, covering a wide range of diagnostic, therapeutic, and surgical procedures for patients of all ages. The department also includes specialized centers, such as the Vision Correction Centre and the Retina & Vitreous Centre.

CLINICAL OPHTHALMOLOGY:

  • Comprehensive eye examinations for all types of vision and ocular health issues.
  • Surgical interventions including cataract removal, glaucoma treatment, and corneal transplants.
  • Laser eye surgery for refractive errors, including LASIK and PRK.
  • Treatment of eye conditions in the Specialty Centers across the Outram Campus, including the National Eye Centre and the Pediatric Ophthalmology Unit.

OPHTHALMIC SURGICAL UNIT:

  • 24-hour specialist care in the ophthalmic surgical unit, providing advanced surgical procedures for complex eye conditions.

VISION CORRECTION UNIT:

  • Offering a range of vision correction services, including prescription glasses, contact lenses, and corrective eye surgeries.

RETINA & VITREOUS CENTRE:

  • Providing specialized care for retinal disorders, including diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and retinal detachment.

FACILITIES AVAILABLE:

  • State-of-the-art ophthalmic surgical equipment.
  • Advanced diagnostic imaging systems, including OCT and fundus photography.
  • Comprehensive patient monitoring and care systems.
