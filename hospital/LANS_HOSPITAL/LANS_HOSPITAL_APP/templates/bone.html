LANS Unit Exchange Hospital boasts an International Standard Bone & Joint Center, which is one of the most advanced and comprehensive facilities in Bangladesh.

Our team of expert Orthopedic Surgeons and Rheumatologists specializes in diagnosing and treating a wide range of musculoskeletal disorders. We are proficient in various surgical and non-surgical procedures, including joint replacement, arthroscopy, and complex fracture management.

All procedures and treatments are conducted using state-of-the-art equipment and the latest medical technologies.

At LANS Unit Exchange Hospital, you not only receive care from some of the best orthopedic and joint specialists, but you also benefit from modern diagnostic techniques and cutting-edge treatment options.

At our Bone & Joint Center, we provide a comprehensive range of orthopedic services, including:

  • Joint Replacement Surgery (Hip, Knee, Shoulder)
  • Arthroscopic Surgery for Sports Injuries
  • Spinal Surgery
  • Complex Fracture Management
  • Minimally Invasive Orthopedic Surgery
  • Hand and Upper Limb Surgery
  • Foot and Ankle Surgery

Services & Facilities:

  • Management of Arthritis (Osteoarthritis, Rheumatoid Arthritis)
  • Osteoporosis Screening and Treatment
  • Comprehensive Care for Back and Neck Pain
  • Sports Medicine and Injury Management
  • Pediatric Orthopedics
  • Rehabilitation and Physical Therapy
  • Bone and Joint Infection Management
  • Custom Orthotics and Prosthetics
  • Bone Density Testing
  • Trauma and Emergency Orthopedic Care
  • Advanced Imaging for Bone and Joint Disorders (MRI, CT, X-ray)
  • Forthcoming: Robotic-Assisted Joint Replacement Surgery

BRBHL Bone & Joint Center ensures:

  • Exceptional orthopedic care in the country.
  • 24/7 emergency coverage for trauma and fractures.
  • Strict adherence to infection-control protocols.

All surgical procedures are performed under anesthesia, ensuring patient comfort and safety.
