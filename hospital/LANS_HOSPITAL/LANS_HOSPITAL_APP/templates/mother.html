Mother & Child Center

"Quality is never an accident. It is always the result of intelligent effort."

  2. Mother & Child Center

LANS Unit Exchange Hospital proudly presents its International Standard Mother & Child Center, a leading facility in Bangladesh dedicated to the comprehensive care of mothers and children.

Our team of expert Obstetricians, Gynecologists, and Pediatricians is committed to providing exceptional care from pregnancy through childbirth and beyond. We specialize in managing high-risk pregnancies, advanced prenatal diagnostics, and neonatal care.

All services are delivered with state-of-the-art technology and in a compassionate, family-centered environment.

At LANS Unit Exchange Hospital, you and your child have access to some of the best maternal and pediatric care specialists, along with the most modern facilities and advanced diagnostic techniques available.

Our Mother & Child Center offers a comprehensive range of services, including:

  • Comprehensive Prenatal Care
  • High-Risk Pregnancy Management
  • Advanced Fetal Monitoring and Imaging
  • Normal and Cesarean Deliveries
  • Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU)
  • Pediatric Surgery
  • Postpartum Care and Lactation Support
  • Child Development and Behavioral Services
  • Family Planning and Reproductive Health

Services & Facilities:

  • Antenatal Classes and Education
  • Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU)
  • Vaccination and Immunization Programs
  • Comprehensive Gynecological Services
  • Newborn Screening and Early Detection Programs
  • Maternal and Child Nutrition Counseling
  • Infertility Treatment and Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART)
  • Pediatric Endocrinology and Diabetes Care
  • Management of Preterm Birth and Low Birth Weight
  • Breastfeeding Support and Counseling
  • Genetic Counseling and Testing
  • Forthcoming: Pediatric Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery

BRBHL Mother & Child Center ensures:

  • The highest standard of maternal and child care in the country.
  • 24/7 availability of emergency obstetric and neonatal services.
  • Strict adherence to infection control and safety protocols.

All deliveries and surgical procedures are performed in a safe, supportive environment, ensuring the best possible outcomes for both mother and child.
