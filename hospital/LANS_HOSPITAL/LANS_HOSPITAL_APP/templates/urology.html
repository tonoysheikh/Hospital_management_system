"Quality is never an accident. It is always the result of intelligent effort."

The Department of Urology at LANS Unit Exchange Hospital offers a wide spectrum of urological services, specializing in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of urinary tract and male reproductive system conditions. The department also includes specialized centers such as the Kidney Stone Treatment Centre and the Men's Health Clinic.

CLINICAL UROLOGY:

  • Comprehensive evaluation and treatment of urological conditions, including prostate disorders, bladder issues, and kidney diseases.
  • Minimally invasive surgical procedures, including laparoscopic and robotic surgeries for urological cancers and other conditions.
  • Management of urinary incontinence and pelvic floor disorders in collaboration with the Women's Health Centre.

UROLOGIC ONCOLOGY UNIT:

  • Specialized care for patients with urological cancers, including prostate, bladder, kidney, and testicular cancers.
  • Multidisciplinary approach involving surgery, chemotherapy, and radiation therapy for comprehensive cancer care.

KIDNEY STONE TREATMENT CENTRE:

  • Advanced treatment options for kidney stones, including extracorporeal shock wave lithotripsy (ESWL), ureteroscopy, and percutaneous nephrolithotomy (PCNL).
  • Preventive care and dietary counseling to reduce the risk of recurrent stone formation.

MEN'S HEALTH CLINIC:

  • Comprehensive services for men's health issues, including erectile dysfunction, infertility, and testosterone management.
  • Preventive screenings and personalized treatment plans for maintaining urological and reproductive health.

FACILITIES AVAILABLE:

  • State-of-the-art urological surgical equipment, including robotic surgery systems.
  • Advanced diagnostic imaging and urodynamic testing facilities.
  • Comfortable outpatient consultation rooms and dedicated recovery areas.
