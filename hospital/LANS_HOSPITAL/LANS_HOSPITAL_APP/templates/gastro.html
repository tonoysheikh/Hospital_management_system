LANS Unit Exchange Hospital has an International Standard Specialized Gastroenterology & Hepatology center, which is largest and Ultramodern center in Bangladesh.

Our team of expert Gastroenterologist routinely diagnose and treat cases of incontinence, severe gastroenterology and hepatology diseases. We’re expert & specialized in various procedures, including advanced diagnostic/ therapeutic endoscopy and liver biopsies.

All procedures and investigations are performed with state-of-the-art facilities.

At LANS Unit Exchange Hospital, you not only get access to some of the best Gastroenterologist & Hepatologist, but also to modern equipment’s and latest diagnostic techniques.

At Gastroenterology & Hepatology center we provide almost all types endoscopic services which include.

  • Diagnostic Oesophago-gastro-duodenoscopy, Colonoscopy and ERCP
  • All therapeutic options for Variceal Bleeding
  • Oesophageal Dilatation and stenting
  • Polypectomy
  • Biliary and pancreatic stone extraction, duct dilatation and stenting
  • Other GI procedures like PEG, Endoscopic, haemostasis, feeding tube insertion etc.
