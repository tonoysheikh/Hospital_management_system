Brain & Spine Center

"Quality is never an accident. It is always the result of intelligent effort."

  1. Home
  2. Brain & Spine Center

LANS Unit Exchange Hospital is proud to offer an International Standard Brain & Spine Center, recognized as one of the leading and most advanced centers in Bangladesh.

Our team of expert Neurosurgeons and Neurologists is dedicated to diagnosing and treating a wide array of neurological and spinal disorders. We specialize in both surgical and non-surgical treatments, including complex brain surgeries, spinal fusions, and minimally invasive procedures.

All procedures and interventions are performed with cutting-edge technology and state-of-the-art facilities.

At LANS Unit Exchange Hospital, you gain access to some of the best specialists in neurology and neurosurgery, alongside the most modern equipment and advanced diagnostic techniques available.

Our Brain & Spine Center provides a comprehensive range of neurological and spinal services, including:

  • Complex Brain Tumor Surgery
  • Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery
  • Management of Spinal Cord Injuries
  • Interventional Neuroradiology
  • Epilepsy Surgery
  • Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS)
  • Cerebrovascular Surgery (Aneurysms, AVMs)
  • Pediatric Neurosurgery
  • Degenerative Spine Disorders

Services & Facilities:

  • Comprehensive Stroke Management
  • Neurotrauma Care
  • Spinal Deformity Correction (Scoliosis, Kyphosis)
  • Peripheral Nerve Surgery
  • Advanced Neuroimaging (MRI, CT, DSA)
  • Functional Neurosurgery
  • Management of Parkinson’s Disease and Movement Disorders
  • Chronic Pain Management (Spinal Cord Stimulation)

BRBHL Brain & Spine Center ensures:

  • World-class neurological and spinal care in the country.
  • 24/7 emergency services for stroke and trauma.
  • Adherence to the highest standards of infection control.

All surgical procedures are performed with the utmost precision, ensuring optimal outcomes and patient safety.
