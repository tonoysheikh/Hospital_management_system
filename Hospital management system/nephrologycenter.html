Nephrology & Urology

"Quality is never an accident. It is always the result of intelligent effort."

  1. Home
  2. Nephrology & Urology

LANS Unit Exchange Hospital is home to an International Standard Nephrology & Urology Center, renowned as one of the most advanced facilities in Bangladesh dedicated to kidney and urinary tract health.

Our team of expert Nephrologists and Urologists specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of a wide range of renal and urological conditions. We offer both medical and surgical solutions, including kidney transplantation, advanced dialysis services, and minimally invasive urological procedures.

All treatments and procedures are performed using cutting-edge technology in a state-of-the-art facility.

At LANS Unit Exchange Hospital, you will receive care from some of the best kidney and urinary tract specialists, alongside access to the latest diagnostic techniques and treatment options.

Our Nephrology & Urology Center offers a comprehensive range of services, including:

  • Kidney Transplantation
  • Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis
  • Management of Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)
  • Treatment of Kidney Stones
  • Minimally Invasive Urological Surgery
  • Treatment of Prostate Disorders
  • Urinary Incontinence Management
  • Bladder and Kidney Cancer Surgery
  • Management of Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs)

Services & Facilities:

  • Comprehensive Renal Function Testing
  • Advanced Imaging for Urological Disorders (Ultrasound, CT, MRI)
  • Endourology and Laparoscopic Surgery
  • Pediatric Nephrology and Urology Services
  • Management of Hypertension Related to Kidney Disease
  • Male Infertility Treatment
  • Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH) Management
  • Vascular Access for Dialysis
  • Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL) for Kidney Stones
  • Urodynamic Testing
  • Genetic Counseling for Hereditary Kidney Diseases
  • Forthcoming: Robotic-Assisted Urological Surgery

BRBHL Nephrology & Urology Center ensures:

  • Exceptional care for kidney and urological conditions in the country.
  • 24/7 emergency services for acute renal and urological conditions.
  • Adherence to the highest standards of infection control and patient safety.

All procedures and treatments are conducted with the utmost precision, ensuring the best outcomes and patient comfort.
