Accident & Emergency

The accident and emergency department (A&E) at BRB Hospitals provides immediate medical attention to critical and life-threatening emergencies, as well as treatment to patients with urgent medical problems.

The average wait time at our A&E is 30 minutes.

Our medical specialists are well-trained to respond to emergencies efficiently and effectively, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Our Services:

  • 24 hour services to all types of emergency patients.
  • Mini Operation Theater with Ventilation Machine for Life Support if needed.
  • All time availability of Consultant & Specialist doctors.
  • Any minor surgery can be done in the Emergency Operation Theater.
  • 5-6 well trained Nurses in each shift.
  • Facility of direct shifting of multiple trauma patients to Trauma Operation Theater via Lift situated within Emergency.
  • Round the clock Ambulance Services:
  • Air Ambulance: Air Ambulance Facilities anywhere of Bangladesh
